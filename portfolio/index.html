A COLLECTION OF EVERY DIFFERENT PART OF MYSELF I'VE LEFT ONLINE SINCE 2008.
A CONTENT CREATION SITE FOR PEOPLE WORKING ONLINE THAT LETS YOU ANONYMOUSLY CREATE VIDEOS. P5JS FILTERS ARE GENERATED TO TRACK AND BLUR YOUR FACE WHILE RECORDING. THE FILTER ALSO PROVIDES AUTONOMY OVER COLOR CHOICE, BLUR, AND FILTER CHOICE. THERE IS ALSO A CHATROOM TO PROVIDE A SPACE TO GATHER, COMMUNICATE, AND CONNECT WITH OTHER MODELS ONLINE.
AN EMOTIONALLY UNAVAILABLE CHATBOT THAT EXPLORES IDEAS OF CONSENT AND DATING ON THE INTERNET. USING MACHINE LEARNING TO TRAIN IT IS ABLE TO HOLD A CONVERSATION WITH YOU WHILE ALMOST COMPLETELY IGNORING RESPONSES BUT RESPONDING TO KEYSTROKE SPEED. IT AGGRESSIVELY HITS ON YOU IN A WAY THAT COULD BE UNCOMFORTABLE. CAN A COMPUTER BE NON CONSENSUAL?
